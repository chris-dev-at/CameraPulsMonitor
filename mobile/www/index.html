How It Works

This app measures heart rate using your camera by detecting color changes in your skin.

Face Mode

Uses AI face detection to find your face, then measures color changes. Works well only with perfect lighting and stable position. Just for fun - not reliable.

Finger Mode

Place your finger over the camera. Uses motion amplification to detect pulses through your skin. Accurate and reliable with good lighting.

Microphone Mode

Place your microphone to your chest and the app will record your heart sounds. (Experimental)

Technical Details

Both face and finger methods use motion amplification and photoplethysmography (PPG) - the same tech as medical pulse oximeters. Blood absorbs green light, so heartbeats cause tiny color changes we detect and amplify. Microphone mode uses basic sound processing.

Accuracy

Not a medical device. Use a medical pulse oximeter for accurate readings.
