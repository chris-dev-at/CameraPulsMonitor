Tips for accurate readings

  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Keep your face centered in the frame
  • Hold still during measurement
  • Stay relaxed and breathe normally

Tips for finger measurement

  • Place finger firmly over camera
  • Keep finger still, don't press too hard
  • Use index finger for best contact
  • Hold still during measurement
